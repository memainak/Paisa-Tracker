PaisaTrack — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 July 2026 · Notice version: 2.0

PaisaTrack ("the App") is operated by Ad-Venture(ad-venture.in) ("we", the Data Fiduciary under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023). Contact: support@ad-venture.in.

1. Summary

PaisaTrack is an offline, on-device expense tracker. There are no user accounts and no sign-in. Every expense, income entry, budget, loan, split, and receipt photo you create is stored only on your device. We do not have servers that hold your financial data, and we cannot see your entries. The one third party that receives any data is Google AdMob, which serves the ads that keep the App free.

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, SMS, contacts, or Gmail. We do not operate analytics or crash-reporting that identifies you. We never sell your data, and your individual expense entries are never shared with any advertising partner.

3. Ads and consent

The App shows ads served by Google AdMob. Before ads load, we show Google's consent form (UMP) where you choose whether ads may be personalised. You can change this choice at any time. If you decline personalisation, AdMob serves non-personalised ads, which still use the advertising ID for frequency capping and fraud prevention. See Google's privacy policy and how Google uses advertising data. You can reset or delete your advertising ID in your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads.

4. Your data and your rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Because your entries live only on your device, you are in direct control of them:

  • Access & correction: view and edit any entry directly in the App.
  • Erasure: delete individual entries in the App, or remove all data by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage in Android Settings → Apps → PaisaTrack → Storage → Clear data. This permanently deletes everything, as we hold no server copy.
  • Advertising data: managed through your device ad settings and the in-app consent choice above.
  • Grievance: Grievance Officer — [REPLACE: officer name], mainak@ad-venture.in. Complaints are acknowledged immediately and answered within 15 days. If unsatisfied, you may approach the Data Protection Board of India.

5. Retention

Your entries are retained on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App. We hold no server-side copy to retain or purge. Advertising data is retained by Google per its own policies.

6. Security

Your data stays within the App's private storage on your device, protected by Android's app-sandbox. We recommend you keep a device screen lock enabled. As data is not transmitted to us, there is no server-side transfer of your financial entries to secure.

7. Third parties

  • Google AdMob — advertising; receives the advertising ID and ad-interaction data. Google privacy policy.

8. Children

PaisaTrack is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect children's data.

9. Changes

We will note material changes in-app and update the notice version above.
