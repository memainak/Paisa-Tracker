2. Consent

At sign-in you give two separate consents: (a) processing needed to provide the service (required), and (b) marketing communications and marketing analysis (optional — the app works fully without it). You can withdraw marketing consent at any time in Settings → Privacy, or via the unsubscribe link in any marketing email. Withdrawal takes effect immediately.

3. Your rights (DPDP Act 2023)

  • Access: Settings → Privacy → "Download my data" gives you a complete copy.
  • Correction: edit your profile and entries in the app.
  • Erasure: Settings → "Delete my account", or the web deletion page. A 7-day grace period applies (sign in again to cancel); all server data, including backups, is purged within 30 days.
  • Grievance: Grievance Officer — [REPLACE: officer name], grievance@ad-venture.in. Complaints are acknowledged immediately and answered within 15 days. If unsatisfied, you may approach the Data Protection Board of India.

4. Retention

Active accounts: retained while active. Deleted accounts: purged as above. Accounts inactive for 24 months receive an email warning, then are deleted 30 days later.

5. Security and storage

All traffic uses TLS 1.2+. Email addresses are encrypted at rest. Our servers and database are located in India. Access to personal data is restricted and logged. In the event of a personal-data breach we will notify the Data Protection Board of India and affected users as required by the DPDP Act and its Rules.

6. Third parties

  • Google Sign-In — authentication only (openid, email, profile scopes).
  • Google AdMob — advertising; receives the advertising ID. Google privacy policy.
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics — crash logs and usage analytics.

7. Children

PaisaTrack is not directed at children. You must confirm you are 18 or older to create an account. We do not knowingly process children's data.

8. Changes

We will notify you in-app of material changes and record the notice version you consented to.
