Delete your PaisaTrack data

PaisaTrack has no accounts and no sign-in. Everything you enter — expenses, income, budgets, loans, splits, and receipt photos — is stored only on your device. We do not keep any copy on our servers, so there is no account to delete and nothing for us to erase on your behalf.

You are in full control of your data. You can remove it at any time, directly on your phone.

Delete individual entries

Open the app and delete any expense, income, loan, or split entry from within it.

Delete everything

To permanently remove all PaisaTrack data from your device, do either of the following:

  1. Clear app data: Android Settings → Apps → PaisaTrack → Storage → Clear data, or
  2. Uninstall the app: long-press the PaisaTrack icon → Uninstall.

Both actions permanently delete all locally stored data. Because we hold no server copy, this fully erases your information.

Advertising data

Ads are served by Google AdMob. To reset or delete the advertising identifier used for ads, go to your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads. You can also change ad personalisation from the consent prompt shown in the app.
